Meaningful sharing

Interactive Embeds

Want to enable the ClipMine experience on your Blogs or Twitter feed? Just embed out Content-Aware Video Player.Its is responsive ad built to work accross different modern browsers and devices.Start by tagging a video of your choice or pick an already tagged one and simply paste the embed code into your website.
