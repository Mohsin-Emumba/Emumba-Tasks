Interactive Embeds

Want to enable the ClipMine experience on your Blogs or Twitter
feed? Just embed out Content-Aware Video Player.Its is
responsive ad built to work accross different modern browsers
and devices.Start by tagging a video of your choice or pick an
already tagged one and simply paste the embed code into your

Smart tagging

Found an interesting point in a video? On ClipMine, addiing
tags is not reserved for the video owners only-any community
member can do it.In addition our smart algorithms can
automatically suggest tags (in private beta) for a wide variety
of videos.
